About Us

Redeeming Time is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering individuals who have served time in jail, prison, probation, or on parole. Our goal is to provide comprehensive support, resources, and opportunities for successful reintegration into society.

Our Mission

At Redeeming Time, our mission is to build a supportive community where individuals who have served time can find the resources, support, and opportunities they need to successfully reintegrate into society and lead fulfilling lives.

Key Features

  • Zoom Calls: Scheduled virtual meetings for community support and resource sharing.
  • Event Calendars: Listings of job fairs, educational workshops, community services, and other relevant events.
  • Progress Tracking: Tools to monitor educational milestones and personal development goals.
  • Resource Directory: Access to local resources and support services.

Video Calls

Our video calls are conducted via Zoom, providing a platform for community support, resource sharing, and group discussions. Below are some categories for different topics you might want to join for video chats:

Categories

  • Job Search Support: Discussions on job hunting strategies, resume building, and interview preparation.
  • Mental Health Support: Access to group counseling sessions and mental health discussions.
  • Educational Workshops: Sessions on continuing education, skill-building, and professional development.
  • Community Services: Information and support on accessing community services and resources.
  • Peer Support Groups: Meetings to share experiences, provide mutual support, and build a community.
  • Legal Advice: Access to legal professionals for advice and information on various legal matters.
