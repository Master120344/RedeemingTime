Supporting Reintegration & Community Empowerment

What is Pathway Forward?

Pathway Forward aims to empower individuals who have served time in jail, prison, probation, or on parole by providing comprehensive support, resources, and opportunities for successful reintegration into society.

Mission Statement

To build a community where every individual has the tools and support needed to forge a positive future.

Core Values

  • Empowerment
  • Community
  • Hope
  • Opportunity

Why Pathway Forward Matters

Reducing Recidivism Rates: Offering resources and support to decrease the likelihood of re-offense

Community Safety: By supporting successful reintegration, we contribute to safer communities

Economic Benefits: Reducing incarceration costs and promoting workforce participation
