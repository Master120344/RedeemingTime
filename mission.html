Our Mission

At Redeeming Time, our mission is to build a supportive community where individuals who have served time can find the resources, support, and opportunities they need to successfully reintegrate into society and lead fulfilling lives.

What We Offer

  • Zoom Calls: Scheduled virtual meetings for community support and resource sharing.
  • Event Calendars: Listings of job fairs, educational workshops, community services, and other relevant events.
  • Progress Tracking: Tools to monitor educational milestones and personal development goals.
  • Resource Directory: Access to local resources and support services.
  • Job Search Assistance: Help with finding employment opportunities and preparing job applications.
  • Mental Health Support: Access to counseling and support groups.
  • Educational Resources: Information on continuing education and skill-building opportunities.

We believe that every individual deserves a second chance, and we are committed to providing the tools and support needed to make that second chance a success.
