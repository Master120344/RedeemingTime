Video Calls

Our video calls are conducted via Zoom, providing a platform for community support, resource sharing, and group discussions. Below are some categories for different topics you might want to join for video chats:

Categories

  • Job Search Support: Discussions on job hunting strategies, resume building, and interview preparation.
  • Mental Health Support: Access to group counseling sessions and mental health discussions.
  • Educational Workshops: Sessions on continuing education, skill-building, and professional development.
  • Community Services: Information and support on accessing community services and resources.
  • Peer Support Groups: Meetings to share experiences, provide mutual support, and build a community.
  • Legal Advice: Access to legal professionals for advice and information on various legal matters.

We believe in the power of community and support. Join our Zoom calls to connect, learn, and grow together.
