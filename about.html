About Us

Empowering Second Chances

About Us

Redeeming Time is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering individuals who have served time in jail, prison, probation, or on parole. Our goal is to provide comprehensive support, resources, and opportunities for successful reintegration into society.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual has the tools and support needed to forge a positive future, regardless of their past. By fostering a supportive community and providing access to vital resources, we aim to reduce recidivism and promote lasting change.

Our Team

Our team is composed of dedicated professionals and volunteers who are passionate about making a difference. With backgrounds in social work, education, mental health, and community development, we bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to our mission.

Get Involved

We welcome individuals and organizations who share our vision to join us in our efforts. Whether you are looking to volunteer, donate, or partner with us, your involvement can make a significant impact.
